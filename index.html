Welcome to Project-404

An intense horror experience. Dive into the depths of fear.

Dark Origins

They called it a signal—buried beneath subspace, impossible to detect. It wasn’t sent. It was waiting. And the moment humanity tuned in, something tuned back.

Governments collapsed in hours. Not because of war—but because people began to speak in voices not their own. Dreams weren’t dreams anymore. They were warnings.

The Descent

First came the red sky. Then the weeping static, bleeding from every screen. Survivors carved symbols into concrete with their bare hands, chanting numbers that were never spoken before.

You are not immune. No one is. But maybe you’re something else now—half-woken, half-severed from whatever calls the dead to walk.

Inside the Madness

There are no monsters. Just reflections of us—twisted by grief, molded by regret. Every “enemy” was someone once. And every time you pull the trigger, the static remembers.

Explore the ruins of a world that tore itself apart without a scream. Piece together lost transmissions. Decode the Signal. Find the Echo Chamber. But do not listen too long…

Step Into the Unknown

“Project-404” is not a remake. It is an original descent into psychological dread, crafted from scratch. Every corner hides lore, and every shadow is a memory with teeth.

This is a living nightmare, stitched from forgotten data and broken minds. It’s not about survival. It’s about remembering what we’ve become.
